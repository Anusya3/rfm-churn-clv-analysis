## Use of This Project (Business & Technical Value)

The core purpose of this project is to provide an end-to-end data science solution that predicts customer churn and performs customer segmentation for an e-commerce business. 

### 1. Proactive Customer Retention (Predictive Power)
Instead of waiting for a customer to stop buying entirely, the machine learning pipeline analyzes historical transactional data to identify early warning signs of defection. It flags high-risk customers dynamically, allowing marketing and operations teams to intervene with targeted promotions before the customer is lost.

### 2. Strategic Customer Segmentation (RFM Framework)
The project groups customers into behavioral segments based on **Recency** (how recently they bought), **Frequency** (how often they buy), and **Monetary** value (how much they spend). This allows businesses to easily separate their most valuable "Champions" from "At-Risk" or "Hibernating" buyers.

### 3. Operational Analytics Interface (Streamlit Dashboard)
The interactive web application acts as a bridge between complex data models and business stakeholders. It enables managers to:
* View macro-level retention and revenue KPIs instantly.
* Filter data by specific customer profiles and risk levels.
* Input manual behavioral data via user-friendly widgets to get real-time risk scores on individual client accounts.
